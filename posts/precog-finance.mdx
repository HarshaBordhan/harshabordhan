### Overview

<br />

<div>
  The Precog finance site is a web application that allows users to track their
  financial goals and progress. It is built using React and Next.js and is
  hosted on Vercel.
</div>

<br />
<br />
<br />

### Features

<br />

- A dashboard that displays a summary of the user's financial goals and progress.

<br />

- A goal tracker that allows users to set and track progress towards specific financial goals.

<br />

- A budget tracker that helps users to track their spending and stick to a budget.

<br />

- A transaction history that allows users to view and categorize their past financial transactions.

<br />
<br />
<br />

### Benefits

<br />

    - The dashboard provides users with a high-level overview of their financial goals and progress, making it easy to stay on track.

<br />

- The goal tracker and budget tracker help users to set and achieve specific financial goals and stick to a budget.

<br />

- The transaction history allows users to view and categorize their past financial transactions, making it easier to track their spending and identify areas for improvement.

<br />

<br />
<br />

### Conclusions

<br />

<div>
  The Precog finance site is a useful tool for anyone looking to track their
  financial goals and progress. Its dashboard, goal tracker, budget tracker, and
  transaction history features provide users with a comprehensive overview of
  their financial situation and help them to make informed decisions about their
  finances. Overall, it is a valuable resource for anyone looking to manage and
  improve their financial situation.
</div>
