### Overview

<br />

<div>
  The Sendy layout site is a single page application that allows users to design
  and customize their own email newsletter templates. It is built using React
  and Next.js and is hosted on Vercel.
</div>

<br />
<br />
<br />

### Features

<br />

- A drag-and-drop editor that allows users to add and rearrange different
  elements (e.g., text blocks, images, buttons) in their email template.

<br />

- A live preview feature that shows users a real-time preview of their email
  template as they design it.

<br />

- A variety of pre-designed templates and layout options that users can choose
  from.

<br />

- A code export feature that generates the HTML code for the email template,
  which users can then copy and paste into their email marketing platform.

<br />
<br />
<br />

### Benefits

<br />

    - The drag-and-drop editor and live preview feature make it easy for users to
    design their email templates, even if they don't have any coding experience.

{' '}

<br />
- The pre-designed templates and layout options provide users with a starting point
and help them to quickly create professional-looking email templates.
<br />
- The code export feature allows users to easily integrate their email templates
into their email marketing platform.
<br />

<br />
<br />

### Conclusions

<br />

<div>
  The Sendy layout site is a useful tool for anyone looking to design and
  customize their own email newsletter templates. Its drag-and-drop editor and
  live preview feature make it easy to design and customize templates, and the
  code export feature allows users to easily integrate their templates into
  their email marketing platform. Overall, it is a valuable resource for anyone
  looking to create professional-looking email templates without the need for
  coding skills.
</div>
